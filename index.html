Brief Description of Application
Simple jump sprint game similar to the loss of internet connection game on Google. With simple sprites and obstacles potentially increasing in difficulty by speeding up the longer you survive. A score tab will also be included to see how far you have made it.

Current Feature list
  • Simple Custom Sprites
  • Simple Obstacles
  • Score counter
  • Simple background and asthetics
I met most of the originally proposed features but failed to implement increased difficulty, score saving for the scoreboard and custom obstacles. I did not expect I would have to have something to trigger the game and I was not able to implement such a feature.

  • More asthetic designs for background
